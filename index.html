Pocket Linesman

The premier all-in-one tool to manage the most important data for your soccer matches.

Distraction Free

Pocket Linesman is designed from the ground up to help you focus on the most important part of your match - the players and their actions. Its clear interface makes it easy for you to see and record the events of a match.

Be sure to try out the Voice Notes feature. Record your observations and never miss a moment.

All-in-One Tool

Pocket Linesman provides you with the most important tools from your referee wallet.
  • A Timer
  • Team Scorecards
  • Tracking Cautionable (Yellow Card) and Send Off (Red Card) offenses
  • Voice Notes
  • Text Notes
  • E-mail match summaries to your referee team or fellow spectators.

Designed for All Environments

Indoors, outdoors, rain, or shine; Pocket Linesman's simple design is made to work in all environments. You will never have to worry about that glare from the sun.
